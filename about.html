About Us

This post was written by The Black Gekko Team

We are average people, not some genetically mutated beings, we began this project on a small but simple idea that we could have a website of our own. We are young people who are willing to do this with limited resources, with little help and a lot of determination. But we dont want to take all the credit to ourselves, we ask others what they want inside of a website and put that in there, we are intersted in what the people want from not limited to a website but a game review site.

Coding and designing a website is rather easy these days if you ignore the long hours of burning your eyes out of your skull, and the thoughts of just banging your head on your desk, but what is hard is idea creation. As an individual you are only one person, and you can only harvest thoughts through your own mental processes. So we look for others ideas or thoughts, what do you think? Or how can i do this better? Are all common questions that allow us to retrive and interpret other peoples point of view, and thus we want to ask you, what are your ideas? let us know so we can follow the idea of making this website a place where ideas about games comes from all over.
